# Research project: Webinar funnel from zero

## Project objective
This repository contains a research project focused on building a webinar funnel from zero. I´ve selected 10  experts to analyze their sales scripts, traffic strategies, and technical setups.

## Methodology
- **Expert selection:** List of practitioners with proven experience making webinar funnels and recognition in their area.
- **Data acquisition:** Using APIs (Supadata) to extract YouTube transcripts and manually collecting LinkedIn content from posts.
- **Version control:** Managed via terminal (PowerShell & Git).

## Expert justification

1. **Russell Brunson** is the co-founder of ClickFunnels and author of 'DotCom Secrets'. His framework for the 'Perfect Webinar' is the most widely used script for structuring sales presentations in digital marketing.
2. **Jason Fladlien** is the founder of Rapid Crushing. He is specialized in the 'closing' phase of the webinar, specifically in how to structure high-ticket offers and manage live Q&A sessions to increase conversion rates.
3. **Dan Henry** is the founder of GetClients.com. His methodology focuses on the 'Minimum Viable Funnel', teaching how to use webinars to sell high-ticket coaching or consulting without complex technical setups.
4. **Jon Penberthy** is the founder of AdSells. He specializes in YouTube Ads, providing the technical data on how to target users based on search intent, which typically results in higher attendance rates for webinars compared to other platforms.
5. **Matt McGarry** is the founder of Newsletter Operator. His data-driven approach shows the correlation between webinar attendance and long-term email engagement, specifically how webinar leads maintain a higher click-through rate (CTR) in newsletters.
6. **Amy Porterfield** is a specialist in digital course launches. Her work provides the strategy for the 'follow-up' sequence, which is the series of automated emails sent after the webinar to capture sales from non-buyers.
7. **Lisa Anne** specializes in Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram). Her contribution to this research is the technical optimization of lead generation forms and ad creatives to reduce the cost per registration (CPL).
8. **Neil Patel** is the co-founder of NP Digital. He provides the omnichannel perspective, focusing on how SEO and content marketing can drive organic traffic to a webinar registration page to reduce dependency on paid advertising.
9. **Casey Zeman** is the founder of EasyWebinar. He was selected to cover the technical infrastructure of 'Evergreen' webinars, which are automated systems that simulate a live event to allow for 24/7 sales operations.
10. **Miles Beckler** is a consultant focused on sustainable online businesses. He provides the 'Bootstrap' perspective, explaining how to integrate affordable or open-source tools to build a professional funnel with minimal initial investment.

## Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md`: List of experts with links, dates, and annotations.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/`: Top-performing posts organized by author.
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/`: Transcripts extracted via API.
- `/research/other/`: Additional strategic materials.
